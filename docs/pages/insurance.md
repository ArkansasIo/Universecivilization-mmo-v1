# Insurance

**Route:** `/insurance`

## Purpose
Manages insurance policies for ships, cargo, and station assets — providing financial coverage against loss, damage, or theft.

## Key Features
- Policy creation per asset (hull, cargo, modules)
- Premium calculation based on risk tier and history
- Claim filing and payout tracking
- Policy tiers: basic, standard, premium, fleet
- Coverage limits, deductibles, and exclusions
- Claim cooldown and fraud detection scoring
