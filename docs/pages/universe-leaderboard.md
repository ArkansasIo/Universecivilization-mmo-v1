# Universe Leaderboard

**Route:** `/universe-leaderboard`

## Purpose
Cross-galaxy rankings comparing players and empires across all servers or galaxies in the universe — the ultimate prestige leaderboard.

## Key Features
- Aggregate rankings across all linked galaxies
- Filters for galaxy, server region, and timeframe
- Global titles and exclusive rewards for top ranks
- Statistical breakdowns per category
- Historical rank tracking and trend graphs
- Hall of Fame for past #1 ranked players
