# Races Explorer

**Route:** `/races-explorer`

## Purpose
An encyclopedia of all playable and NPC races in the universe — their lore, traits, ships, and faction affiliations.

## Key Features
- Race catalog with lore descriptions and visuals
- Trait breakdown: bonuses, penalties, special abilities
- Race-specific ships, technology trees, and architecture
- Faction alignment and historical relationships
- Starter system and homeworld locations
- Comparison tool for race stats side-by-side
