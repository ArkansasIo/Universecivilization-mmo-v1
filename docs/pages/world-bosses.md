# World Bosses

**Route:** `/world-bosses`

## Purpose
Tracks powerful NPC boss entities that spawn in specific systems — coordinating player efforts to defeat them for rare rewards.

## Key Features
- Active and upcoming boss spawns with countdown timers
- Boss detail panels: level, health pool, abilities, loot table
- Player contribution tracking and damage leaderboard
- Group/raid formation and join requests
- Spawn conditions and trigger mechanics
- Loot distribution policy and reward history
