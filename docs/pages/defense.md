# Defense

**Route:** `/defense`

**Purpose:** Construct and manage planetary defense systems.

**Key Features:**
- Build defensive structures on owned planets
- Each defense type has unique damage profiles and targeting priorities
- Defenses automatically engage attacking fleets

**UI Sections:**
- **Defense Types** — catalog of available defenses (missile batteries, laser turrets, shield generators, etc.)
- **Planet Selector** — choose which planet to view/edit defenses
- **Damage Profiles** — per-defense stats showing damage, range, accuracy, and target priorities
- **Construction Queue** — defenses currently under construction with progress and costs
