# Officers

**Route:** `/officers`

**Purpose:** Recruit, manage, and assign officers to ships and fleets.

**Key Features:**
- Browse available officers and recruit them
- Assign officers to command positions on ships
- Officers provide stat bonuses and special abilities

**UI Sections:**
- **Officer Roster** — list of owned officers with name, rank, portrait, and specialization
- **Recruitment Pool** — available officers for hire with costs and stats
- **Assignment Panel** — drag-and-drop interface to assign officers to fleet/ship slots
