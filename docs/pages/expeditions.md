# Expeditions

**Route:** `/expeditions`

**Purpose:** Send fleets on deep-space expeditions with discovery-based outcomes.

**Key Features:**
- Explore uncharted space sectors for resources, artifacts, and dangers
- Expedition outcomes are influenced by fleet composition and officer skills
- Discover rare loot, technologies, and alien encounters

**UI Sections:**
- **Expedition Map** — sector map with available expedition destinations and danger ratings
- **Fleet Assignment** — select which fleet to send and configure supplies/duration
- **Discovery Outcomes** — results screen showing artifacts, resources, and events encountered
- **Expedition Log** — history of past expeditions with discoveries and losses
