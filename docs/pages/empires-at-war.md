# Empires at War

**Route:** `/empires-at-war`

## Purpose
A live war dashboard showing active conflicts between empires and alliances — battle fronts, casualties, and war progress.

## Key Features
- Active wars list with belligerents, duration, and status
- Theater-of-war map with front lines and contested systems
- War score tracker and victory condition progress
- Casualty counts (ships, stations, troops)
- Battle log: recent engagements and outcomes
- Surrender, ceasefire, and peace negotiation actions
