# Home

**Route:** `/`

**Purpose:** Landing/marketing page for Universe Civilization. Uses `useAuth` to redirect authenticated users to `/dashboard`.

**Key Features:**
- Hero section with animated cosmic background, empire branding, and primary CTA buttons (Login, Register, Instant Demo)
- Three "Pillars of Empire" cards: Military Might, Economic Power, Diplomatic Mastery
- Systems showcase grid highlighting Fleet Command, Research, Crafting, Alliances, Colonies, Market, Officers, World Bosses
- Warfare section with real-time combat engine overview, espionage, and salvage mechanics
- Civilization building section covering colony management, technology trees, and infrastructure
- Universe statistics display (30 universes, 300 realms, 50K+ active commanders)
- Roadmap timeline with 4 phases (Foundation, Conquest, Dominion, Legacy)
- Developer team spotlight and newsletter signup
- Scroll-based sticky navigation with transparent-to-solid background transition
