# Intel

**Route:** `/intel`

## Purpose
A centralized intelligence dashboard gathering reconnaissance data, intercepted signals, and analyzed threat assessments about other players and factions.

## Key Features
- Intel feed: decoded messages, fleet sightings, activity reports
- Threat assessment matrix per faction or player
- Intel tier system: unverified, corroborated, confirmed
- Scan history and anomaly reports
- Intel-sharing with alliance members
- Source tracking and intel reliability scoring
