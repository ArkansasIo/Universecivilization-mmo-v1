# Campaign

**Route:** `/campaign`

**Purpose:** Story-driven campaign mode with sequential chapters and missions.

**Key Features:**
- Progressive chapter structure with branching paths
- Each mission has unique objectives, enemies, and rewards
- Track campaign progress and replay completed missions

**UI Sections:**
- **Chapter Select** — map/chapter overview showing completed, active, and locked chapters
- **Mission List** — missions within the selected chapter with difficulty stars and rewards
- **Rewards Panel** — guaranteed and bonus rewards for each mission completion
