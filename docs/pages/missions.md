# Missions

**Route:** `/missions`

**Purpose:** Deploy and manage fleet missions across the galaxy.

**Key Features:**
- Browse available missions with requirements and rewards
- Assign fleets to missions with duration and success probability
- Track active missions and collect rewards on completion

**UI Sections:**
- **Available Missions** — list of missions with difficulty, duration, and reward preview
- **Active Missions** — currently deployed missions with time remaining and status
- **Mission Detail** — expanded view showing requirements, enemy forces, and reward breakdown
