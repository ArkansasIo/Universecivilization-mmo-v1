# Bounties

**Route:** `/bounties`

## Purpose
A bounty board where players can place and claim bounties on other players, NPCs, or specific targets — PvP and PvE hunting.

## Key Features
- Browse active bounties by target, reward, and faction
- Place a bounty with credit amount and conditions
- Claim a bounty and track hunting progress
- Bounty history: placed, claimed, expired
- High-value and faction-sponsored bounty highlights
- Reputation impact for placing and claiming bounties
