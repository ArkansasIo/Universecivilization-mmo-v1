# Leaderboard

**Route:** `/leaderboard`

## Purpose
Rankings of players and alliances across competitive categories — combat, economy, exploration, and overall power.

## Key Features
- Category tabs: combat rank, wealth, fleet power, exploration, influence
- Paginated rankings with player name, score, and tier
- Weekly, monthly, and all-time leaderboard periods
- Alliance rankings alongside individual
- Click-through to player/alliance profiles
- Achievement badges and top-rank rewards display
