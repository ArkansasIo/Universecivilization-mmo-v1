# Training Center

**Route:** `/training-center`

**Purpose:** Train and improve ground unit quality and capabilities.

**Key Features:**
- Enlist and train new units
- Training queue with configurable batch sizes
- Each training tier improves unit stats

**UI Sections:**
- **Training Queue** — ordered list of units in training with progress bars and time remaining
- **Trainable Units** — available unit types to train, showing cost, duration, and resulting stats
- **Unit Upgrades** — upgrade existing units to higher tiers with cost breakdown
