# Universe 3D

**Route:** `/universe-3d`

## Purpose
A WebGL-based 3D visualization of the universe allowing free-form camera exploration of galaxies, star clusters, and deep-space structures.

## Key Features
- Full six-degrees-of-freedom camera controls
- Procedurally generated star fields and nebula effects
- Click-to-inspect celestial objects (stars, planets, anomalies)
- Time-lapse mode showing celestial motion
- Performance scaling (LOD switching, particle limits)
- VR/head-tracking support (where available)
