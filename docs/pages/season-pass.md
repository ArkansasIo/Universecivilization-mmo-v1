# Season Pass

**Route:** `/season-pass`

## Purpose
The seasonal battle pass system — players earn XP and unlock tiered rewards by completing challenges and playing actively during a season.

## Key Features
- Free and premium track with tiered rewards
- Daily and weekly challenge list with XP values
- Season progress bar and remaining time
- Reward preview for upcoming tiers
- Season pass purchase and upgrade options
- Past season archive with exclusive rewards
