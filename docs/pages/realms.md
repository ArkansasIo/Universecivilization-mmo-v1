# Realms

**Route:** `/realms`

## Purpose
Manages player-created realms — persistent, customizable regions of space that function as independent territories with their own rules and governance.

## Key Features
- List of owned and discovered realms
- Realm creation wizard (naming, borders, governance model)
- Realm permissions and member roster management
- Resource and construction tracking within the realm
- Diplomacy actions with other realms
- Realm history log (founded, wars, treaties)
