# Ground Combat

**Route:** `/ground-combat`

**Purpose:** Resolve planetary ground invasions between attacker and defender ground forces.

**Key Features:**
- Assign ground units to invasion or defense
- Auto-resolve combat with phase-by-phase results
- Battle report with casualties and outcome

**UI Sections:**
- **UnitAssignRow** — row component for assigning a unit type to attack or defense
- **PlanetCard** — summary card for the target planet showing defenses, garrison, and morale
- **BattleReportPanel** — detailed combat results including rounds, casualties, and victor
