# Black Market

**Route:** `/black-market`

## Purpose
An illegal, reputation-gated trading network offering contraband goods, restricted technology, and covert services — accessible only to those with the right connections.

## Key Features
- Hidden listings that rotate on a timer
- Reputation/standing requirements per vendor
- Contraband items: illicit tech, stolen goods, forbidden artifacts
- Risk of scan inspection and faction penalties
- Anonymous transactions (untraceable payments)
- Faction-aligned black market brokers
