# Marketplace

**Route:** `/marketplace`

## Purpose
The central hub for buying and selling goods, ships, modules, and services between players and NPC factions.

## Key Features
- Buy and sell orders with quantity and price
- Category browsing: ships, weapons, resources, blueprints, services
- Search and advanced filtering (rarity, tier, faction)
- Order history and transaction log
- Price charts and market trend data
- Quick-sell and bulk-listing tools
