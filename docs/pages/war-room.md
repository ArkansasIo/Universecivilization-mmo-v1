# War Room

**Route:** `/war-room`

**Purpose:** Strategic command center for monitoring active wars and conflicts.

**Key Features:**
- Overview of all active conflicts involving your faction
- Track war progress, casualties, and territorial changes
- Declare war or negotiate peace

**UI Sections:**
- **Active Conflicts** — list of current wars with opponent, duration, and status
- **War Status** — detailed view of selected conflict including battle history, score, and losses
- **War Actions** — buttons for peace offers, reinforcements, or war declarations
