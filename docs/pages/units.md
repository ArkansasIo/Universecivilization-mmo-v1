# Units

**Route:** `/units`

**Purpose:** Manage military ground units and their assignments.

**Key Features:**
- View all owned units with detailed stats
- Assign units to planetary garrisons or invasion forces
- Track unit experience and veterancy

**UI Sections:**
- **Unit List** — table/cards showing unit type, count, stats, and current assignment
- **Stats Panel** — attack, defense, health, speed, and special abilities per unit type
- **Assignment Controls** — assign or transfer units between planets and task forces
