# Store

**Route:** `/store`

## Purpose
The premium in-game shop where players purchase cosmetic items, convenience boosts, and exclusive content using premium currency.

## Key Features
- Catalog: ship skins, decals, emotes, titles, boosters
- Featured and limited-time offers
- Premium currency purchase and balance display
- Purchase history and inventory management
- Gift purchases to other players
- Bundles and discount promotions
