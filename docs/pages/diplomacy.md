# Diplomacy

**Route:** `/diplomacy`

## Purpose
Manages formal relationships between empires, alliances, and independent factions — treaties, pacts, and negotiations.

## Key Features
- List of all diplomatic contacts with relationship score
- Treaty proposals: NAP, trade pact, mutual defense, research sharing
- War declarations and surrender negotiations
- Trust and reputation tracking per faction
- Diplomatic mail and proposal history
- Third-party mediation and peace conferences
