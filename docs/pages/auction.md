# Auction

**Route:** `/auction`

## Purpose
A timed auction house where rare ships, modules, blueprints, and collectibles are bid on by players across the galaxy.

## Key Features
- Live and upcoming auction listings
- Bid placement and auto-bidding system
- Countdown timers with bid-sniping prevention
- Auction categories: ships, weapons, artifacts, titles
- Watchlist and bid history per item
- Seller dashboard with reserve prices
