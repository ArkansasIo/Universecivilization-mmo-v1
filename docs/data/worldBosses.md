# World Bosses

**File:** `src/data/worldBosses.ts`

## Purpose
World boss encounter definitions — powerful open-world raid bosses with scaling difficulty and rewards.

## Key Exports
- `worldBosses: WorldBossData[]` — Array of world boss definitions with id, name, title, tier, level, health, maxHealth, shield, maxShield, armor, damage, status (active | defeated | upcoming | legendary), location, participants, timeRemaining, rewards (metal, crystal, deuterium, darkMatter, credits, XP, loot), abilities, weaknesses, difficulty, and respawnTime.
- Bosses include Inferno Colossus, The Leviathan, Void Reaper, Frost Giant, Nova Phoenix, Eternal Necromancer, Colossus Omega, The Unbound, Aetherial Titan, and Dread Overlord.
